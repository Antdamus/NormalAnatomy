CORE_FACTS_USED:

- Colloid cysts are benign developmental lesions and the most common mass of the foramina of Monro.
- Colloid cyst is a circumscribed, nonenhancing lesion characteristically arising from the anterior roof of the third ventricle.
- Machine oil-like contents appear hyperattenuating on CT and low signal intensity on T2-weighted images.
- In adults, the most common mass of the foramen of Monro is colloid cyst; in children, SEGA is the key competing diagnosis.
