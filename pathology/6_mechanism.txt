MECHANISM DEPTH RULE (MANDATORY)

For major disease processes in which imaging depends on the underlying tissue injury pattern

When source support is sufficient, the explanation MUST be built from the ground up using this ladder:

Гўв‚¬Вў Identify the relevant cells, layers, ducts, vessels, tissue compartments, or fascial planes
Гўв‚¬Вў Example: acinar cells, zymogen granules, ducts, lysosomes, bowel wall layers, portal venous channels

Гўв‚¬Вў Example: obstruction, ischemia, enzyme activation, infection, inflammatory injury, vascular compromise

Cellular / microanatomic consequence

Гўв‚¬Вў This level is REQUIRED when it is central to the disease mechanism
Гўв‚¬Вў Example: compartment mixing, enzyme activation, vascular leak, endothelial injury, fat necrosis, epithelial destruction, pressure elevation, ischemic devitalization

Гўв‚¬Вў Example: one enzyme activating others, continuing secretion into a disconnected system, inflammatory mediator cascade, ongoing leakage, superinfection of dead tissue

Гўв‚¬Вў How the above appears on CT / MR / US / radiograph

This ladder is mandatory for:

Гўв‚¬Вў Complication mechanisms
Гўв‚¬Вў Imaging discriminator mechanisms
Гўв‚¬Вў Processes where superficial phrasing would lose the true causal logic

HISTOLOGY / MICROANATOMIC DETAIL RULE (MANDATORY WHEN CENTRAL)

Гўв‚¬Вў Which tissue compartment is destroyed, edematous, necrotic, hemorrhagic, fibrotic, or obstructed
